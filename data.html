Data Analysis

The First For Data Communication

Data analysis is a crucial step on ecological studies in order to reach the main information which will be the base for the further steps, including data plot, statistical analysis, reports and the taking of decisions.

The team involved on the data analysis counts with more then 10 years of experience on ecological data analysis covering such areas:

  • Abiotic Data
  • Molecular and Functional Diversity
  • Carbon Cycles
  • Climate Data

Information and Concision.

Data plot is a very informative tool and synthesize a big amount of information in a simple and direct representation. Thus, the nature of the dataset must be understood and then the best representation is applied.

Static plots are broadly used for different media such as reports, websites, presentations and papers.

The use of Interactive Plot put the data visualization on a next level where the dataset can be accessed in a easy and intuitive way. Its application covers different data with support to API, georreferenced Data and Integration with Map Services.

From Static to Dynamic

Combining the usability of webpages and the power of different packages, the use of dynamic documents have big advantages over other digital formats. The use of dynamic documents in HTML format allows to integrate different langages and communicate in a very straightforward way.
